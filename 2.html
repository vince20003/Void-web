VAXER FPS

Lightweight Performance Boost Modpack ( Non Survival ) 1.21.5

Non Survival Lightweight FPS Boost Modpack
Description

VAXER FPS is a lightweight, performance-focused modpack for Minecraft 1.21.5, built on Fabric Latest. Designed from the ground up for maximum efficiency, it removes all survival content and gameplay additions to focus purely on boosting frame rates, reducing stutter, and improving chunk loading and overall responsiveness.

Ideal for PvP, multiplayer servers, and exploration, VAXER FPS delivers smooth, stable performance even in high-entity environments or at extended render distances. It installs quickly, runs cleanly on low-end and high-end systems alike, and offers a distraction-free experience built entirely around speed and stability.

Compatibility

Minecraft: 1.21.5
Mod Loader: Fabric Latest
Renderer: LTw Renderer or Mobileglues
Launcher: Pojavlauncher, ZalithLauncher, MinecraftLauncher.
Modworks: Serverside, Clientside, Hybrid.
